Chiffon Cake by Menome

Description

What's a Chiffon Cake?

Ingredients

  • 5 eggs
  • 134g sugar
  • 96g flour
  • 56g oil
  • 81g milk
  • ¼teaspoons cream of tartar or ½teaspoons vinegar
  • ½teaspoons baking powder
  • ¼teaspoon salt
  • ½tablespoons vanilla

Steps

  1. Separate the eggs yolks from the whites.
  2. Add half the amount of sugar to the egg yolks and whisk directly.
  3. Heat the milk and oil slightly in the microwave for 30 seconds or a minute.
  4. Then Add to the eggs and sugar mixture.
  5. Add Vanilla
  6. Sift the flour, baking powder,salt,and mix them well and leave them aside.
  7. In another bowl
  8. Whisk the egg whites and cream of tartar then gradually add the remaining sugar, and whisk until stiff peaks
  9. Fold in 1/4 of the egg whites into the egg yolk batter .Then gently fold in the rest of the egg whites until no white streaks remain.
  10. Pour the batter into cake pan and bake at 150 Celsius for about 1 hour.
  11. Invert and let cool completely
